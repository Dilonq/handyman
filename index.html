About Me

With years of experience in home maintenance and repair, I pride myself on delivering high-quality craftsmanship and reliable service. Whether it's a leaky faucet or a full room painting project, I treat every home as if it were my own.

Our Services

Appliance Services

Professional installation and replacement for major household appliances. We handle kitchen disposals, dishwashers, and ovens, as well as laundry units, range hoods, and window A/C systems.

Electrical & Lighting

From minor repairs to modern upgrades. We specialize in light fixture installations, ceiling fan mounting, and outlet or switch replacements to keep your home safe and bright.

Assembly & Mounting

Precision mounting and professional assembly. We handle everything from home theater setups (TVs and soundbars) to furniture assembly, door installations, and outdoor play equipment.
